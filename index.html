Product Info

At Anney Fragrances, every scent is a reflection of identity and emotion. Our 50mL oil-based perfumes contain 30% fragrance oil for long-lasting wear, praised by many for their staying power.

Each scent is hypoallergenic, available in 31 unique variants, and offers a perfect balance of quality and affordability. Discover the fragrance that feels uniquely yours.

Our Story

Anney Fragrances began with a simple idea: scent has the power to move, to transform, and to stay with us. What started as a quiet passion grew into a commitment to creating meaningful fragrances that connect with people.

Guided by creativity and care, we continue to craft perfumes that speak to the soul and celebrate the beauty of individuality.

About Us

We are an independent fragrance house dedicated to the timeless craft of perfumery. With every creation, we aim to capture emotion in a bottle, offering something personal, poetic, and unforgettable.

Our team blends creative vision with deep technical knowledge to create scents that are both artful and wearable. We invite you to explore and find the fragrance that becomes your signature.
